How the system behaves

Invoice postingDr Student Debtor, Cr Fee Revenue. Draft invoices do not affect balance until posted.
Receipt postingDr Bank, Cr Student. Only posted receipts reduce the invoiced balance.
Refund paymentDr Student / Refund Control, Cr Bank after approval and payment.
Reversal controlPosted items are not deleted. They are reversed with a reason and audit trail.
Bank balancesEvery posted receipt increases selected bank. Paid refunds reduce the selected bank.
Ledger visibilityStudent ledger shows invoices, receipts, refunds and reversals in one place.
